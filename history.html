Our Story

The journey of JAKS – from concept to creation

Back when I was pursuing Electrical Engineering at Carmel College, we were asked to come up with ideas for our final engineering project. I was honestly confused at first — but then, the idea of an automatic shoe cleaner sparked in my mind.

I shared it with my professors, and to my surprise, they encouraged me to build a prototype and present it. At the time, I had no clue where to start, though I had fragments of ideas floating in my head. I spoke to one of my best friends about it, and he was incredibly supportive.

What followed were days — and then months — of trial, error, late nights, and learning. Eventually, I managed to put together a working prototype. It wasn’t perfect, but it was real.

I submitted the prototype as part of my final project, and to my amazement, it didn’t just work — it impressed. The professors were intrigued by the functionality and vision. With my friends by my side, we decided not to let this remain just a college project.

We took it further. We built a brand, a website, and most importantly, a purpose.

What started from a humble cardboard box ended up being featured in Time Magazine — and today, JAKS stands as a symbol of innovation, simplicity, and the future of daily living.

And that’s how it all began. - KASINATH P
